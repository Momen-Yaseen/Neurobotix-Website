Reinventing Brain Aneurysm Interventions.

Our startup aims to revolutionise the treatment of cerebrovascular aneurysms through an innovative combination of robotics, magnetic guidance, and AI. Our technology is designed to improve the precision, safety, and efficiency of endovascular procedures while reducing their complexity. By providing intelligent robotic assistance for catheter navigation, we enhance patient outcomes and improve the working conditions of neurointerventionalists, enabling safer and more precise interventions.
