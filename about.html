About us

Reinventing Interventional Neuroradiology

A MedTech startup at a late R&D stage, founded in France by two researchers.

Neurobotix Technologies is a MedTech startup in the surgical sector, currently at a late R&D stage, founded by two researchers, with the ambition to become a global leader in the treatment of brain aneurysms, one of the world's leading causes of mortality and disability. Neurobotix Technologies enables fully autonomous navigation of micro-catheters in the cerebrovascular network using robotics, magnetic guidance, and AI.
